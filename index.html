Rehabilitation Services

Advanced neuro and sports rehabilitation to restore function, independence, and quality of life.

Expert Rehabilitation

Comprehensive Rehabilitation
for Every Condition

Our rehabilitation services bring together neurological, orthopaedic, and sports rehabilitation under one roof. With a team of 15+ specialised professionals, we deliver the best possible recovery outcomes.

🧠 Stroke Rehabilitation

Structured neurological rehab focusing on movement restoration, balance re-training, and functional independence.

🫀 Parkinson's Disease

Evidence-based physiotherapy to improve gait, reduce fall risk, and maintain mobility through progressive training.

🦽 Spinal Cord Injury

Comprehensive rehab from acute injury management to long-term functional training and community reintegration.

👶 Cerebral Palsy

Gentle, progressive physiotherapy for children and adults — improving motor function and daily living activities.

🏃 ACL & Ligament Rehab

Structured protocols for safe return-to-sport, focusing on strength, proprioception, and biomechanical correction.

⚡ Overuse & Running Injuries

Biomechanical analysis, load management, and progressive loading to resolve chronic athletic pain.

🏀 Sport-Specific Conditioning

Cricket, football, basketball, and marathon-specific rehab bridging clinical recovery to field readiness.

📈 Return-to-Play Testing

Objective functional testing to ensure safe, confident return to training and competition.

🦴 Joint Replacement Rehab

Post-op knee, hip, and shoulder replacement recovery with phased mobility and strength restoration.

🩹 Fracture & Post-Surgical

Guided tissue healing, scar mobilisation, and progressive weight-bearing protocols.

🔄 Chronic Pain Management

Hands-on therapy, neural glides, and graded exposure to restore pain-free movement patterns.

📏 Postural & Ergonomic

Workplace and lifestyle adjustments combined with targeted corrective exercise programs.

🛤️ Your Recovery Journey

1. Assessment & Diagnosis

Thorough clinical evaluation, movement screening, and goal setting with our lead physiotherapist.

2. Personalised Plan

Customised rehab protocol combining manual therapy, targeted exercise, and progressive loading.

3. Guided Treatment

Hands-on therapy, electrotherapy, hydrotherapy, and supervised functional movement sessions.

4. Independence & Maintenance

Transition to home programmes, strength maintenance, and periodic check-ins to prevent recurrence.
