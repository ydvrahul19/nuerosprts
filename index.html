Common Questions

Physiotherapy FAQs

Everything you need to know before starting physiotherapy.

The number of sessions depends on your condition, severity, and how your body responds. Acute injuries typically resolve in 6–10 sessions, while chronic or complex conditions may require 12–20+ sessions. After your initial assessment, Dr. Ruchit Shah will give you a clear treatment timeline and expected outcome milestones.
No referral is needed. You can book directly with us. However, if you have recent scans (X-ray, MRI, or CT), do bring them — they help us plan a more targeted treatment programme for you.
Physiotherapy should not be painful. Some techniques — like joint mobilisation or deep soft tissue work — may cause mild temporary discomfort, but our therapists work within your comfort level at all times. Most patients feel significant relief within the first 2–3 sessions.
Wear loose, comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the affected area. Bring any relevant medical reports, scans, or prescription medicines. Arrive 10 minutes early for your first visit to complete a brief intake form.
Yes, we offer home visit physiotherapy for patients who are unable to travel due to post-surgical recovery, severe mobility limitations, or other health conditions. Please call or WhatsApp us at +91 94295 54422 to arrange a home visit.
Absolutely. Neuro Sports Centre has dedicated neuro physiotherapists specialising in stroke rehabilitation, Parkinson's disease, spinal cord injury, and cerebral palsy.
